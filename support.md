## Frequently asked questions

### Getting started

**How do I add a habit?**
On the **Today** screen tap **Add habit**, or pick a template from the empty-state on first launch. You can add up to 10 habits on the free plan.

**How do I check in?**
Tap a habit row once. For habits that allow multiple completions per day (e.g., 8 glasses of water), each tap adds one. Long-press to undo your most recent check-in.

**How do I see my history?**
Tap a habit's title to open its detail page. You'll see your current streak, weekly progress, and a 30 / 90 / 365-day heatmap.

### Reminders & notifications

**Why didn't I get a reminder?**

1. **System notifications enabled** for Habion (iOS Settings → Notifications → Habion; Android Settings → Apps → Habion → Notifications).
2. **In-app notifications on** (Habion → Settings → Notifications).
3. The habit has a **reminder time set** (open the habit → Reminder section).
4. The day is in the habit's **schedule**.

If all of these are correct and reminders still don't fire, please email us with your device + OS version.

**Can I set different times for the same habit?**
Yes — for habits with multiple completions per day, Habion learns your usual check-in times and schedules reminders around them. You can also pin a fixed reminder time from the habit's detail page.

**How do I turn off daily motivation pings?**
Settings → Notifications → toggle off. Habit-specific reminders continue independently.

### Customisation

**How do I change to 12-hour or 24-hour time?**
Settings → **Time format**. The time picker and all displayed reminder times follow your choice.

**How do I change the app language?**
Settings → **Language**. English, Türkçe, Français, Español and Deutsch are supported. No restart required.

**Can I use a custom icon or color?**
Yes — when creating or editing a habit, pick from the icon grid and use the colour wheel.

### Data & privacy

**Does Habion work offline?**
Yes. The app stores everything locally — no internet is required for any feature.

**Can I sync between devices / iPad / Android?**
Not yet. Habion is intentionally local-first for privacy. Cross-device sync is on the roadmap for a future Premium release.

**How do I back up or export my data?**
Manual export isn't available yet — it's planned as part of the upcoming Premium release. Until then, please don't uninstall the app if you want to keep your history.

**How do I delete all my data?**
Uninstall the app. Because data lives only on your device, removing the app removes everything.

**Where can I read your privacy policy?**
See the [Privacy Policy](./privacy).

### Premium

**Is Premium available?**
Not yet. The Premium tier is in development and will unlock:

- Unlimited habits (the free plan is capped at 10)
- Home screen widgets
- Custom themes
- Data export

We'll announce timing in the app's release notes and on the store listing.

**Has anything been charged to my account?**
No. Habion has not enabled any in-app purchases yet. If you see an unexpected charge labelled "Habion", please email us so we can investigate.
